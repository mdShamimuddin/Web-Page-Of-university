About IBS

The Institute of Bangladesh Studies (IBS) is a constituent Institute of the University of Rajshahi, the second oldest and largest University of Bangladesh (with 8 different faculties and 48 different departments, about 30,000 students and 1000 teachers) founded by an Act of the East Bengal Legislature in 1953 on a large area of 1000 acres of land at the eastern side of Rajshahi City. IBS is the oldest and largest of the 5 research Institutes of the university. The Institute is run through same committees and bodies such as the Institute Committee, Board of Studies (BoS) and Board of Governors (BoG) with broad frame work of policies, rules and regulation framed by the university.
